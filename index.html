Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the ClipMine experience in your blog or Twitter feed? Just embed our Content-Aware Video Player. It is responsive ad built to work across different modern browsers and devices. Start by tagging a video of your choice or pick an already tagged one and simply paste the embed code into your website.

Smart Tagging

Found and interesting point in a video? On ClipMine, adding tags is not reserved for the video owners only - any commnuity member can do it. In addition our smart algorithms can automatically suggest tags (in private beta) for a wide variety of videos.
